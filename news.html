    Sachin Tendulkar's Records

    Sachin Tendulkar, widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket, holds numerous records in the sport:

    • Most Runs in International Cricket: Sachin has scored the most runs in both Test and One Day International (ODI) cricket.
    • Most Centuries: He holds the record for the most centuries in international cricket.
    • Highest Number of ODI Runs: Sachin holds the record for the highest number of runs scored in ODI cricket.
    • First Batsman to Score a Double Century in ODIs: Sachin was the first player to achieve this milestone.
    • Most Test Matches Played: He has played the highest number of Test matches in cricket history.
    • Most ODI Matches Played: Sachin also holds the record for playing the most ODI matches.

    These are just a few of the many records held by Sachin Tendulkar, showcasing his unparalleled skill and longevity in the sport.
