    About Sachin

    Sachin Tendulkar, born on April 24, 1973, in Mumbai, India, is a former Indian international cricketer and a former captain of the Indian national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket.

    Records

    During his illustrious career, Sachin Tendulkar set numerous records in international cricket, including the highest number of runs scored in both Test and One Day International (ODI) cricket. He also holds the record for the most centuries scored in both formats of the game.

    Career

    Sachin Tendulkar's cricketing career spanned over two decades, during which he achieved remarkable success and left an indelible mark on the sport. From his debut in 1989 to his retirement in 2013, Tendulkar's journey is a testament to his dedication, talent, and love for the game.
